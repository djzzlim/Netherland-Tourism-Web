Weerribben-Wieden National Park

The largest freshwater wetland in northwest Europe, the Weerribben-Wieden National Park is a tranquil and enigmatic landscape of canals, ponds, and lakes. A treasure trove of biodiversity may be found throughout its 10,000 marshy hectares of peatlands, bogs, and reed beds that have been home to many orchid species for millennia. Enjoy it all while cycling along one of the many beautiful trails.

Explore National Park

Artis Royal Zoo

a botanical garden and zoo in the heart of Amsterdam. It is the fifth-oldest zoo in the world and the oldest in the Netherlands. In addition to the zoo, Artis also houses the Groote Museum, Micropia, an aquarium, a planetarium, and an arboretum.

Diergaarde Blijdorp

also referred to as Rotterdam Zoo. It is one of the oldest zoos in Netherlands. There is also a store, a large number of restaurants, and an information centre provided inside the zoo area.

Burgers' Zoo

Eco-displays at Burgers' Zoo replicate distinctive nature reserves as realistically as naturally as possible, frequently allowing animals to coexist in relative freedom with hundreds of plant species.

The Anne Frank House
The Anne Frank House was established on 3 May 1957 in cooperation with Otto Frank, Anne Frank’s father. We are an independent non-profit organisation that runs a museum in the house where Anne Frank went into hiding and we try to increase awareness of Anne’s life story all over the world.
